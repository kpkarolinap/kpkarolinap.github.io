Hi, I'm Karolina! I'm a rising 4th year undergraduate at the University of Washington, studying Computer Science.


I was born in Warsaw, Poland, and have grown up around the Seattle area after moving to the United States when I was 2 years old.


This summer I have returned to Google as an Associate Product Manager intern in New York, and this fall I will be returning to Facebook as a Software Engineering intern in Menlo Park, CA.


I previously served as a Google Student Ambassador for my university and an officer for the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM) within the University of Washington Computer Science Department.


I am a UW CSE scholarship recipient, Washington State Opportunity Scholar, and NASA Space Grant Scholar at the University of Washington.
